About Me

I'm Dominick Girard, an upcoming game developer passionate about gameplay systems, user experience, and building polished interactive experiences.

Ever since I was young, I’ve been fascinated by how games are made. I started creating small projects and experimenting with game engines, which pushed me to pursue game development seriously.

My experience spans Unity, Unreal Engine, C#, C++, UI/UX implementation, gameplay design, and working within structured pipelines. I’ve contributed to multiple projects that helped grow my technical problem‑solving skills, teamwork, communication, and overall design sensibilities.

I love crafting smooth player experiences—whether through intuitive UI, responsive controls, or immersive systems. I’m always learning, experimenting, and improving with every project I take on.

I’m driven, adaptable, and eager to collaborate with others to create meaningful and exciting interactive experiences.
